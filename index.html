About

Dr. K.T. Joshi is a highly accomplished and dedicated chemistry lecturer who has been passionately teaching the subject since 1992 at Navjivan Science College in Dahod. With an extensive academic background and a wealth of experience, he has become a respected figure among both his colleagues and students. In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Dr. Joshi serves as a PhD guide, mentoring and supervising the research endeavors of two bright students under his guidance. His specialized area of expertise lies in the fascinating field of coordination chemistry, where he has made significant contributions through his innovative research.

Driven by his insatiable curiosity and deep understanding of the subject, he continually explores the intricacies of coordination compounds and their applications in various industries. Dr. Joshi's passion for teaching, coupled with his commitment to advancing the boundaries of knowledge through research, inspires and motivates his students to excel in their academic pursuits. His dedication and expertise have undoubtedly played a pivotal role in shaping the next generation of aspiring chemists and researchers.
